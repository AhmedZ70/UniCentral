Help & Feedback

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of any page. You'll need to provide your email address and create a password. Once you verify your email, you'll have full access to all features.

How do I add courses to my profile?

You can add courses to your profile by visiting the "Courses" page, finding a course you're taking, and clicking the "Add to My Courses" button. You can also manage your courses from the "My Courses" page.

How do I connect with classmates?

Once you've added courses to your profile, you can visit the "My Classmates" page to see other students who are taking the same courses. From there, you can send study buddy requests to connect with them.

How do I write a review?

To write a review, navigate to the course or professor page you want to review. Click the "Write a Review" button and fill out the review form. Your review will be posted once submitted and will help other students make informed decisions.

How do I plan my courses?

Use the "Course Planner" feature to plan your academic journey. You can add courses to different semesters, track your progress, and ensure you're meeting all your degree requirements.

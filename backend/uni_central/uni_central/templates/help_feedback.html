Frequently Asked Questions

What is UniCentral?

UniCentral is a platform designed to help students make informed decisions about their courses and professors. We provide course reviews, professor ratings, and a course planning tool to help you plan your academic journey.

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Register" button in the top right corner of the page. You'll need to provide your email address and create a password. We recommend using your university email for verification purposes.

Can I submit a review anonymously?

Yes, your reviews will be displayed anonymously to other users. However, you need to be logged in to submit a review to prevent spam and ensure quality content.

How can I use the Course Planner?

Our Course Planner allows you to organize courses by semester. Simply navigate to the Course Planner page, add a semester, and then add courses to that semester. You can also upload your transcript to automatically import your courses.

Are the professors notified when I leave a review?

No, professors are not notified when reviews are posted. All reviews are anonymous to encourage honest feedback while maintaining professionalism.

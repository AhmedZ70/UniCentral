About UniCentral

UniCentral is an innovative web platform designed to simplify the course registration process for students by integrating essential resources into one user-friendly platform.

Our Mission

We aim to empower students by providing comprehensive course information, professor reviews, workload assessments, and schedule planning tools in a single, intuitive interface.

Meet Our Team

UniCentral was developed by a dedicated team of Computer Science students from the University of Utah. Our team brings together diverse skills and perspectives to create the best platform for students.

About UniCentral

UniCentral is an innovative web platform designed to simplify the course registration process for students by integrating essential resources into one user-friendly platform.

Our Mission

We aim to empower students by providing comprehensive course information, professor reviews, workload assessments, and schedule planning tools in a single, intuitive interface.

The Problem We're Solving

College students face significant challenges when planning their academic journey:

  • Scattered information across multiple platforms
  • Limited access to candid course reviews from peers
  • Difficulty finding study partners for challenging courses
  • Complex degree planning with prerequisite requirements
  • Lack of transparency about professor teaching styles and course workloads

These challenges can lead to poor course selections, academic stress, and potentially delayed graduation timelines.

Our Solution

UniCentral brings together all the tools students need in one comprehensive platform:

Course Reviews

Honest, anonymous reviews from peers who've taken the courses.

Professor Ratings

Comprehensive professor evaluations on teaching style, availability, and more.

Course Filtering

Powerful filters to find courses by department, difficulty, rating, and other criteria.

Course Planner

Visual semester planning tool that helps map out your entire degree path.

Study Buddies

Connect with classmates for collaborative studying and course assistance.

Discussion Boards

Course-specific forums where students can ask questions and share insights.

Technologies We Use

Backend
